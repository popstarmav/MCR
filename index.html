About $MCR

The payout consists of 25% of profits, split evenly: 12.5% goes to holders as a reward for their support, and 12.5% supports scam victims, aligning with $MCR’s mission to fight rug pulls and foster a fair community. If the goal isn’t met, no payout occurs for that quarter, and profits roll over to the next evaluation period, ensuring the project’s sustainability. This quarterly payout rule also applies to any other memecoins we launch in the future. Whether ist’s a new coin inspired by $MCR’s vision or a different memecoin project, the same principle of quarterly distributions—contingent on meeting a target—will guide our approach, creating a consistent and transparent reward system across our ecosystem.
