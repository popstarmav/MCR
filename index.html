About $MCR

Welcome to Memecoin Reserve ($MCR), inspired by the Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) mission to cut waste and boost efficiency. Launched on Pump.fun and powered by Solana, $MCR tackles rug pulls by sharing 25% of profits as Meme Dividends, dynamically adjusted based on market cap. From this, the creator takes a fixed $70,000 as a fair reward, with the remainder split equally—12.5% to holders (proportional to holdings) and 12.5% to scam victims (based on approved claims). If profits are too low to support the $70,000 cut, dividends are paused. The other 75% is retained for sustainability (development, marketing, operations), mirroring DOGE’s goal of streamlining for growth. This quarterly payout system aligns with our efficiency-driven ethos, creating a transparent reward ecosystem across all memecoins we launch.
